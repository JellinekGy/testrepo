Welcome

This is the home of J.R.R. Tolkien, father of high fantasy, inventor of constructed languages, writer, poet, philologist, and academic.

Achievements

Born in South Africa, his life was full of adventure even as a baby. He had been bitten by a baboon spider before the age of three. Perhaps this inspired him later to create the giant spiders in his books. He saw World War I from the front lines, and participated in the Battle of the Somme. This experience helped him write his memorable battle scenes. For his outstanding achievements in literature, he was appointed Commander of the Order of the British Empire by Queen Elizabeth II.
